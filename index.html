JESS LEUNG


Hello, I'm Jess. I'm a pop/jass singer.

ABOUT ME

Born and raised in Richmond Hill, Jessica Leung is an up and coming singer-songwriter in the Toronto scene.

Jessica's jazz pop music has been showcased at the Mod Club, Revival Club, and with the Raptors 905 team and Art of Time Ensemble.

Jessica is the 2016 winner of the Landmark Events Showcase Festival in Toronto and recently performed two Canadian Music Week at The Supermarket and C'est What.

Jessica is now passionately working on her EP, set to come out in early 2017. Welcome to her website, the hub for all media and information about upcoming performances!
